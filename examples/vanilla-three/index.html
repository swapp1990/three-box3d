three-box3d

3D physics for three.js, powered by box3d (Erin Catto).

The production-hardened three.js & React Three Fiber layer: typed handles, island-aware sleep, InstancedMesh transform sync, and a fixed-timestep runtime — proven on a live deployed app. Every page below is a self-contained index.html + main.ts with a View source link.
